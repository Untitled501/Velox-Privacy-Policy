Velox Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

Velox collects your email address for account creation and authentication via Firebase Authentication.

How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely for authentication. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

App preferences and usage patterns are stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud Key-Value Storage. Task and point data is stored locally on your device.

Third Party Services

Velox uses Firebase Authentication by Google for account management. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how Firebase handles data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at veloxappsupport@gmail.com
