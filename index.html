Velox Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

Velox collects your email address for account creation and authentication via Firebase Authentication. We also collect your display name if you choose to provide one during onboarding.

How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely for authentication and account management. Your display name is used to personalize your experience within the app. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

Tasks, points, and schedule data are stored locally on your device using Core Data. App preferences, usage patterns, and settings are stored locally and synced via iCloud Key-Value Storage. Pattern learning data used for scheduling suggestions expires automatically after 14 days of inactivity.

Biometric Data

Velox offers optional Face ID authentication to lock the app. Biometric data is processed entirely by iOS and is never accessed or stored by Velox.

Notifications

Velox may send local notifications to remind you of upcoming tasks and scheduled sessions. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the app.

Spotlight Search

With your permission, Velox indexes task and point titles in iOS Spotlight so you can find them from your home screen. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third Party Services

Velox uses Firebase Authentication by Google for account management. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how Firebase handles data.

Children's Privacy

Velox is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at veloxappsupport@gmail.com
